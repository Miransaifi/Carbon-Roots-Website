About

Boutique, expert-led, and commercially grounded.

Carbon Roots combines carbon market analysis, automation thinking, and financial modelling discipline to provide pre-feasibility support that is faster and more affordable than engaging a large consultancy too early.

The founder brings hands-on experience in carbon market analysis and sequestration-led financial modelling, with academic grounding in Business Management (University of Edinburgh) and Environment and Sustainable Development (UCL).
