Carbon Roots engine

Structured system workflow in seven concise steps.

  1. 01

    Intake

    Standardize project inputs, boundaries, and assumptions. Flag missing data early.

  2. 02

    Feasibility framing

    Define no-project baseline, ARR pathway, and key land, species, and timeline constraints.

  3. 03

    Carbon estimation

    Estimate indicative sequestration and draft issuance curves using conservative assumptions.

  4. 04

    Risk adjustments

    Apply buffer, leakage, delay, and P50/P90 style logic to stress-test projections.

  5. 05

    Financial modelling

    Translate credits into low, mid, and high revenue scenarios with NPV, IRR, and payback.

  6. 06

    Investment screen

    Classify outcomes as investable, conditionally viable, or not yet ready.

  7. 07

    Output

    Deliver clear recommendations and practical next-step pathways.
