Carbon Roots Engine

A seven-step workflow from intake to recommendation.

  1. 01

    Project intake & structuring

    Standardize inputs, define boundaries, and surface weak data early.

  2. 02

    Baseline & feasibility framing

    Set no-project baseline, ARR pathway, and key land/species/timeline constraints.

  3. 03

    Carbon quantification (indicative)

    Estimate sequestration with conservative assumptions and generate issuance curves.

  4. 04

    Risk & adjustment layer

    Apply leakage, delays, buffer logic, and P50/P90 issuance perspectives.

  5. 05

    Financial modelling

    Translate credits to low/mid/high revenue scenarios and run NPV, IRR, payback.

  6. 06

    Investment-readiness screen

    Classify as investable, conditionally viable, or not yet ready.

  7. 07

    Output & recommendation

    Deliver clear outputs and practical next-step pathways.
